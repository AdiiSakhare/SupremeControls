# Supreme Controls Website Strategy

## Project Overview

Build a premium corporate website for Supreme Controls.

Supreme Controls specializes in:

- Industrial Automation
- PLC Solutions
- SCADA Solutions
- Automation Consultancy
- Electrical Control Systems
- PCC Panels
- MCC Panels
- APFC Panels
- PLC Control Panels
- Custom Control Panels
- Security Systems
- Smart Infrastructure Solutions
- Turnkey Engineering Projects

The website should position Supreme Controls as a modern engineering company capable of delivering reliable industrial automation and electrical control solutions at scale.

The final experience should feel closer to Siemens, Schneider Electric, ABB and premium enterprise software companies rather than traditional engineering websites.

---

# Core Objectives

The website must:

- Build trust immediately
- Showcase technical expertise
- Demonstrate engineering capability
- Present products professionally
- Communicate execution strength
- Generate enquiries through phone, email and WhatsApp
- Strengthen premium brand perception

# Visual Direction

Inspired by:

- Siemens
- Schneider Electric
- ABB
- Honeywell
- Stripe
- Linear
- Ramp

Characteristics:

- Large whitespace
- Strong typography
- Clean grids
- Minimal visual noise
- Premium imagery
- Elegant composition

---

# Image Direction

Preferred imagery:

- Industrial facilities
- Automation systems
- Control rooms
- Electrical infrastructure
- Manufacturing environments
- Engineers at work
- Project execution scenes

People should primarily appear as Indian professionals where relevant.

Avoid:

- Generic handshake photos
- Unrealistic AI people
- Outdated factories
- Low-quality stock photography

Optional:

Natural orange accents may appear in equipment, safety gear or environmental elements.

---

# Motion Direction

Use:

- Fade reveals
- Staggered content appearance
- Smooth hover effects
- Section transitions
- Number counters
- Smooth scrolling

Animation duration:

300ms–800ms

Avoid:

- Heavy parallax
- Floating elements
- Excessive motion
- Distracting animations

Motion should feel premium and intentional.

---

# Contact Strategy

No contact forms anywhere on the website.

Use only:

- Phone
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Office Address

All contact methods should be clickable.

---

# Final Experience Goal

Every visitor should leave with the impression:

"Supreme Controls is a highly capable engineering company with the expertise, infrastructure and execution strength required to deliver complex industrial automation and electrical control solutions with confidence."
