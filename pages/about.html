Our Story

From a single workshop to full-scale industrial engineering.

Supreme Controls was founded in 2010 in Pune with a focused purpose — to bring engineering precision to industrial automation. What began as a specialist control panel fabrication workshop has grown into a multi-discipline engineering firm serving manufacturing plants, infrastructure projects, and process industries across India.

Over 15 years, we built deep capability across six engineering disciplines — industrial automation, PLC & SCADA systems, building automation, agriculture automation, security solutions, and automation consultancy — accumulating experience across demanding industrial environments where systems must perform reliably, continuously, and safely.

Today, our team of engineers and technicians manages projects from initial scoping through commissioning and post-handover support. Our works facility at MIDC Pune fabricates control panels to IEC and IS standards in-house — giving us direct quality control at every stage.
