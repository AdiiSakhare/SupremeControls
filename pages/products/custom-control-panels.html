Panel Types

Any panel, any application.

Process Control Panels

Combined power, control, and instrumentation panels for process plant applications — integrating PLCs, field I/O, drives, and process instruments.

Marshalling Cabinets

Field cable marshalling panels with terminal strips, cable segregation, and junction arrangements for instrument and control signal consolidation.

Machine Control Panels

Electrical control panels for individual machines or production equipment, typically wall-mounted or machine-mounted with compact footprint.

Sub-Distribution Boards

Secondary power distribution boards for machine shops, utility areas, and building sub-circuits with outgoing MCB protection and metering.

Hazardous Area Panels

Flameproof (Ex-d) and increased safety (Ex-e) certified panels for Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous area installations in chemical and oil & gas facilities.

OEM / Machine Builder Panels

Custom panels manufactured to OEM machine builder specifications — consistent quality, documentation, and delivery for production runs.
