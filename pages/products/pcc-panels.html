PCC Panels

Main power distribution built for industrial reliability.

Power Control Centres are the primary incoming power distribution boards at the main switchboard level. They receive power from the utility or transformer, provide metering and measurement, and distribute power to downstream MCCs, sub-distribution boards, and large loads through outgoing feeders with appropriate protection devices.

Our PCC panels are designed and fabricated to IEC 61439 standards with fully type-tested components from approved manufacturers. Every panel is designed from your single-line diagram and site-specific requirements.

Key Features

Built for demanding power distribution.

Fully Form-4B Construction

Individual compartmentalisation of busbars, functional units, and cable termination areas for safe working and fault containment.

Electronic Trip Units

Microprocessor-based protection relays with adjustable overload, short circuit, earth fault, and neutral protection settings.

Metering & Measurement

Digital multifunction meters measuring voltage, current, power factor, kWh, kVAR, and power quality parameters with communication outputs.

Copper Busbars

Electrolytic grade copper busbars, sized to current density, with PVC sleeving, heat-shrink insulation, and silver-plated joints for low-resistance connections.

Surge Protection

Type 1 and Type 2 surge protection devices as standard, protecting downstream equipment from transient overvoltages and lightning surges.

Full FAT Documentation

Insulation resistance test, Hi-pot test, functional testing, and verification against approved drawings before despatch.
