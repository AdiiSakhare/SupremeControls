PLC Panels

Control hardware and field I/O in a single integrated panel.

A PLC panel brings together the programmable controller, I/O modules, power supplies, signal conditioning, marshalling terminals, and HMI display into a single, factory-tested enclosure. Delivered with the programme installed and tested against your functional specification, ready for site connection and commissioning.

We design PLC panels for all major automation platforms — Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Schneider Electric, Mitsubishi, and others — with the panel hardware and software both engineered by the same team to avoid integration gaps.

Key Features

Hardware and software in one engineered package.

Multi-Platform Support

Panels engineered for Siemens S7-1200/1500, Allen-Bradley CompactLogix/ControlLogix, Schneider M340/M580, and Mitsubishi iQ-R series.

Integrated HMI

Touch HMI screens mounted on the panel door for local operator interface, alarm display, and manual control — pre-configured and tested before despatch.

Fieldbus Communication

Profibus DP, Profinet, Modbus RTU/TCP, EtherNet/IP, and DeviceNet communication modules integrated and configured for your network architecture.

Marshalling Terminal Strips

All field cables terminated on dedicated marshalling terminals with wire ferrules, labels, and clear separation of AI, AO, DI, DO, and communication signals.

Programme Loaded & Tested

PLC programme and HMI configuration loaded, simulated, and factory tested against functional specification before panel despatch to site.

Redundancy Options

Redundant CPU, power supply, and communication modules available for high-availability applications where process continuity is critical.
