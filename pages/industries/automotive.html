Automotive

Precision automation for high-volume production environments.

Automotive manufacturing operates at volumes and precision levels where control system performance directly determines product quality and throughput. Assembly line synchronisation, robot cell integration, and production quality gate automation require PLC systems that are fast, deterministic, and well-integrated with the broader plant network.

We have delivered electrical and automation engineering for automotive assembly plants and Tier-1 component suppliers — understanding the production pressures and documentation standards of this sector.
