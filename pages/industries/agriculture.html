Agriculture & Agro-processing

Control systems for operations where reliability directly affects crop value.

In agricultural and agro-processing environments, control system failures carry direct product losses — irrigation system faults affect crop yield, cold storage control failures cause produce spoilage, and grain processing downtime reduces throughput at peak harvest periods.

We deliver automation and electrical engineering for irrigation pump stations, cold storage facilities, grain silos, and agro-processing plants — with awareness of the seasonal operational patterns and power supply conditions typical of rural and semi-urban agricultural sites.

Irrigation pump automation with soil moisture, level, and timer-based control
Cold storage control — temperature monitoring, compressor sequencing, alarm systems
Grain processing automation — conveyor, elevator, dryer, and silo level control
MCC and PCC panels for agricultural pumping stations and processing facilities
Remote monitoring and mobile alerts for unmanned or remotely operated sites
