Overview

Security infrastructure designed for industrial environments.

Industrial sites present specific security challenges — large perimeters, high-value assets, round-the-clock operations, and multiple entry points that require controlled, auditable access. Off-the-shelf residential or commercial security products rarely meet the operational requirements of manufacturing plants or infrastructure facilities.

We design, supply, and install integrated security systems that are engineered for the actual conditions of your site — incorporating the right mix of surveillance, access control, and detection technology to provide comprehensive protection without operational disruption.

CCTV and IP surveillance system design, installation, and commissioning
Access control systems — biometric, RFID, card-based, and visitor management
Intrusion detection and perimeter alarm systems for industrial sites
Integrated command centre with unified security management software
System integration with existing plant infrastructure and ERP systems
