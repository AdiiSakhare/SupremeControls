Overview

Objective automation advice — not tied to any platform or vendor.

Automation investment decisions are long-term commitments. The wrong technology choice, an underspecified system, or an unrealistic implementation plan can result in significant cost overruns and operational disruption.

Our consultancy service provides independent technical input from engineers with hands-on experience across multiple automation platforms and industry sectors — helping you make informed decisions before committing to a project direction.

Automation feasibility studies and technology assessment for planned investments
Platform selection guidance — Siemens, Rockwell, Schneider, ABB, Mitsubishi comparison
Existing system audit — identify gaps, risks, and improvement opportunities
Project scoping and specification development — basis of design and functional requirements
Automation roadmap development — phased implementation plans aligned to budget and operations
Consultancy Areas

Where we add independent value.

Feasibility Assessment

Technical and commercial feasibility review of proposed automation projects — confirming what is achievable, realistic risks, and expected return.

Technology Selection

Platform-agnostic comparison of automation hardware and software options against your specific technical requirements, budget, and long-term support considerations.

System Audit

Assessment of existing automation and control systems — identifying reliability risks, obsolescence, performance bottlenecks, and security gaps.

Roadmap Planning

Phased automation implementation plan with defined milestones, budget allocation, and sequencing that minimises production impact during rollout.

Specification Development

Functional requirements specification, basis of design, and technical scope documents — the foundation for accurate tendering and contractor selection.

Owner's Engineer

Independent technical oversight of automation projects managed by third parties — ensuring specifications are met and your interests are protected throughout delivery.
