Overview

Automation systems engineered for your process.

We design, build, and commission complete industrial automation systems — from single-machine control to plant-wide integrated systems. Every project begins with understanding your process: the materials, the sequences, the safety requirements, and the operational constraints that define what the system must do.

Our teams work across discrete manufacturing, batch processing, and continuous process environments. We select and integrate the right combination of PLCs, drives, sensors, safety systems, and communication infrastructure to meet your production and operational objectives.

Complete system design from process concept to electrical schematics and control architecture
PLC programming, HMI development, and SCADA integration across all major platforms
Control panel fabrication, testing, and factory acceptance testing before site delivery
Site installation, loop checking, commissioning, and site acceptance testing
Operator training, as-built documentation, and post-commissioning support
Capabilities

What we deliver.

Discrete Automation

Conveyor systems, robotic cell integration, assembly line control, and packaging automation for discrete manufacturing environments.

Process Control

PID loop tuning, batch sequencing, continuous process control, and recipe management for chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing.

Motion Control

Variable frequency drive integration, servo and stepper motor control, multi-axis coordinated motion for precision manufacturing applications.

Safety Systems

Safety PLC programming, safety relay integration, emergency stop systems, and SIL-rated safety function design to IEC 61508 / 62061 standards.

System Integration

Third-party equipment integration, fieldbus networking (Profibus, Profinet, Modbus, EtherNet/IP), and existing system upgrades.

Operator Training

Structured training programmes for plant operators, maintenance technicians, and engineering staff covering system operation and fault diagnosis.
