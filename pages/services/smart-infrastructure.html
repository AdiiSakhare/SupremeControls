Overview

Intelligent building systems engineered for efficiency.

Supreme Controls designs and integrates building automation systems that bring together HVAC, lighting, access control, fire alarm, and energy management into a single intelligent control layer. We work on both new construction projects and retrofit installations across commercial, industrial, and institutional facilities.

Our BMS solutions deliver measurable results: reduced energy consumption, lower operational costs, improved occupant comfort, and real-time visibility into building performance. Every system is designed with remote monitoring capability and scalability for future expansion.

BMS design and integration — centralised monitoring and control of all building systems
HVAC automation — temperature, humidity, and ventilation control with energy optimisation
Lighting control systems — zone-based, occupancy-triggered, and daylight-harvesting solutions
Energy monitoring and reporting — real-time consumption dashboards and demand management
Retrofit and upgrade of legacy building systems to modern, networked automation platforms
Capabilities

What we deliver.

BMS Integration

Centralised building management platforms that unify HVAC, lighting, power, fire, and access systems — with operator dashboards and mobile access.

HVAC Control

AHU, FCU, chiller, and VRF system automation with occupancy scheduling, demand-based ventilation, and PID temperature control loops.

Lighting Automation

Zone-based DALI / DALI-2 and 0–10V lighting control, motion and occupancy sensor integration, scene programming, and daylight harvesting.

Energy Management

Sub-metering, demand monitoring, peak load management, and automated reporting to track and reduce energy costs across the facility.

Access & Fire Integration

Integration of access control, CCTV, and fire alarm systems into the BMS for unified monitoring, event logging, and emergency response coordination.

Remote Monitoring

Cloud-connected and on-premise SCADA dashboards providing real-time building status, alarm notifications, and performance analytics accessible from anywhere.
