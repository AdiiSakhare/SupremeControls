Overview

Automation systems designed for agricultural precision.

Supreme Controls applies industrial automation expertise to the specific demands of agricultural operations. We design and commission control systems for irrigation networks, pump stations, greenhouse climate control, fertigation, and water treatment facilities — covering both small-farm applications and large-scale agricultural infrastructure.

Our systems are built for reliability in field environments: weather-resistant panels, remote SCADA access via cellular or LoRa networks, and control logic that handles pump sequencing, flow balancing, and schedule-based irrigation without requiring constant operator intervention.

Drip and sprinkler irrigation automation — zone scheduling, flow monitoring, and valve control
Pump station control — multi-pump sequencing, VFD integration, and dry-run protection
Greenhouse climate management — temperature, humidity, CO₂, and shade control
Fertigation control — nutrient dosing, EC/pH monitoring, and injection sequencing
Remote monitoring via SCADA — real-time field data, alarms, and control from any device
Capabilities

What we deliver.

Irrigation Control

Multi-zone drip, sprinkler, and micro-irrigation automation with soil moisture feedback, weather-based scheduling, and flow-rate monitoring.

Pump Automation

Single and multi-pump control panels with VFD drives, automatic duty-standby selection, pressure-based regulation, and dry-run / overload protection.

Greenhouse Automation

Climate control for polyhouses and glasshouses — fan-pad cooling, heating, roof vent, shade net, and supplemental lighting managed by PLC with SCADA interface.

Fertigation Systems

Automated nutrient injection, EC and pH monitoring, venturi and dosing pump control, and batch recipe management for precision crop nutrition.

Remote SCADA

GSM / LoRa / Ethernet connected SCADA systems for remote field monitoring, pump and valve control, alarm SMS notifications, and data logging.

Water Treatment Automation

Control systems for water filtration, chlorination, UV treatment, and RO plants serving agricultural and rural water supply applications.
