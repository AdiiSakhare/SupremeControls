IEC & IS Standards

All panels designed and fabricated to IEC 61439, IS 13947, and applicable Indian and international standards for low voltage switchgear assemblies.

Premium Branded Components

Circuit breakers, contactors, and protective devices sourced exclusively from Siemens, ABB, Schneider Electric, L&T, and other approved manufacturers.

Factory Acceptance Testing

Every panel undergoes full FAT before despatch — insulation resistance, Hi-pot, functional testing, and verification against approved drawings.

Complete Documentation

As-built drawings, FAT reports, bill of materials, O&M manuals, and spare parts list — complete documentation handover with every panel.

Thermal Management

Properly sized cable ducts, defined busbar current densities, ventilation calculations, and where required — forced cooling and anti-condensation heaters.

IP-Rated Enclosures

Enclosure selection from IP31 indoor-rated to IP65 weatherproof, matched to site conditions — dusty, wet, outdoor, or hazardous area classifications.
